Who We Are

Addiction Stress Center has been owned and operated by Scott Robinson Jr. LAC since 1999.

He has been counseling people with addiction issues for over 40 years.

Scott brings experience, care, and compassion to every patient because he realizes that healing needs a holistic approach and is willing to work with every person to give them the best experience and opportunity for healing and healing the families touched by addiction.
